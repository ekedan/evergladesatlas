Everglades Atlas: Selections from the Interior / Elite Kedan
Everglades Atlas: Selections from the Interior, is a publication of selected work and images by Elite Kedan from Everglades National Park and surrounding areas, collected and produced during the artist in residence program (AIRIE)

This book was compiled to document a month spent in the Everglades experiencing the variation of landscapes, environments, structures and spirits of the territory. The result is a kind of archive, catalog and general mound of the photographs, ephemera and drawings produced during that time.
First version published November 2015, 246 x 189 mm, 736 pages
